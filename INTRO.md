# unitree-g1-dev-copilot

`unitree-g1-dev-copilot` is a lightweight skill for AI-assisted Unitree G1 development. It uses a local bundle of official G1 docs to answer questions about SDK, DDS, ROS2, service interfaces, motion development, and the head RealSense camera without making up unsupported details.

这是一个给 Unitree G1 开发场景准备的轻量 skill。它把本地整理的 G1 官方文档作为主要依据，帮助 AI 助手更可靠地回答 SDK、ROS2、DDS、服务接口、运动开发和深度相机相关问题。
